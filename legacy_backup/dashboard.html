Help Center

How to Use This Dashboard

  1. Upload your sales data (.csv or excel) using the Sync Data button.
  2. Ensure your file has a Column for Transaction ID and a Column for Product Name.
  3. Watch the system automatically find hidden purchasing patterns!

Understanding the Metrics

  • Rules Found: The total number of valid product combinations discovered in your data.
  • Top Associations: Items frequently bought together.
  • Confidence % (Bars): How likely it is that a customer will buy the second item. Example: 85% means 85 out of 100 people who bought the first item also bought the second.
  • Inventory Movement: Your absolute best-selling individual item categories.

What Are Lost Sales Opportunities?

The Gap (Missed %): This highlights times a customer bought the first item, but didn't buy the second item that usually goes with it.

Action: Click "Create Bundle" to make a marketing strategy for these two items so you don't lose that sale next time!
